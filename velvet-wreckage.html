About Velvet Wreckage

Elegance after impact.

Velvet Wreckage lives in the tension between softness and damage: black silk against broken stone, gold dust over ash, tenderness held inside a sharp silhouette.

The collection is built for mysterious editorials, intimate objects, and fragments that feel discovered rather than displayed. Nothing shouts. Everything lingers.

Luxury in the ruins. Beauty with a bruise.
