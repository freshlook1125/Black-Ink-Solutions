About Black Ink

A publishing-house feeling for emotional work.

Black Ink is a refined creative home for collections that feel intimate, literary, and visually unforgettable. It pairs elegant serif typography with handwritten energy, warm ivory space, and fine gold marbling lines.

Each collection has its own atmosphere while remaining connected by an editorial sense of care: soft luxury, emotional truth, and artful restraint.
